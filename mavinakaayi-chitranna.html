Mavinakaayi Chitranna - "ಮಾವಿನಕಾಯಿ ಚಿತ್ರಾನ್ನ" - Raw Mango Rice

Raw mango rice is a popular South Indian dish, especially prevalent during the summer season when green, unripe mangoes are abundant. Known by various regional names—such as Mavinakaayi Chitranna (Kannada), Mamidikaya Pulihora (Telugu), and Mangai Sadam (Tamil)—this dish is celebrated for its tangy, sour flavor and the way it combines simple ingredients to create a vibrant, aromatic meal.
